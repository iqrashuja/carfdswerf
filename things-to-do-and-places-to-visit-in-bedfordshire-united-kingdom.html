Things to do and places to visit in Bedfordshire, United Kingdom

The Shuttleworth Collection

If you have a passion for vintage aircraft and automobiles, then a visit to The Shuttleworth Collection in Bedfordshire is an absolute must. Nestled in the picturesque countryside, this collection houses an impressive array of historic airplanes, cars, and motorcycles dating back to the early 1900s. Walking through the hangars feels like stepping back in time, with each artifact meticulously preserved and showcasing the evolution of transportation over the decades.

The collection also features regularly scheduled airshows where visitors can witness these magnificent aircraft in action, soaring gracefully through the skies above Bedfordshire. Whether you're a history enthusiast, an aviation aficionado, or simply looking for a unique and unforgettable experience, The Shuttleworth Collection offers a one-of-a-kind glimpse into the golden age of transportation. Plan your visit to explore these vintage treasures and immerse yourself in the bygone era of aviation and motoring excellence.

Wrest Park

Wrest Park is a majestic English heritage site located in Bedfordshire. The grandeur of the 92-acre garden and grounds make it a perfect destination for history buffs and nature enthusiasts alike. The sprawling landscape features a stunning array of French and Dutch-style gardens, showcasing intricate statues, peaceful water features, and vibrant flower beds.

As you wander through Wrest Park, you'll be transported back in time to the 18th century, where you can marvel at the impressive architectural features of the grand mansion. Whether you're strolling through the formal gardens or exploring the wooded areas, Wrest Park is sure to captivate visitors with its unparalleled beauty and historical significance.

Stockwood Discovery Centre

Stockwood Discovery Centre is a captivating cultural and historical attraction situated in Luton, Bedfordshire, United Kingdom. This center offers visitors a unique opportunity to explore the rich heritage and diverse exhibitions showcasing art, social history, and the natural world. With beautifully landscaped gardens and interactive displays, Stockwood Discovery Centre is a must-visit destination for individuals of all ages looking to immerse themselves in a wealth of knowledge and beauty.

Visitors to Stockwood Discovery Centre can admire a vast collection of carriages, including the majestic Mossman Collection, which displays stunning horse-drawn vehicles ranging from ornate state coaches to practical carts used in everyday life centuries ago. The Mossman Gallery also hosts a fascinating array of carvings, sculptures, and art pieces, providing a glimpse into the artistic talents of different cultures throughout history. Additionally, the outdoor Discovery Garden at Stockwood Discovery Centre showcases various horticultural techniques and plants, offering a peaceful and educational experience for those interested in gardening and nature.

Stockwood Discovery Centre



Dunstable Downs

Dunstable Downs is renowned for its breathtaking panoramic views and is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The vast expanse of lush greenery and rolling hills provide the ideal setting for picnics, hikes, and leisurely strolls. Visitors can relish in the fresh air while taking in the stunning vistas of the surrounding countryside.

For those seeking a bit of adventure, Dunstable Downs is a prime spot for paragliding and kite-flying due to its open landscape and consistent winds. It's a exhilarating experience to soar through the air and feel the rush of adrenaline as you take in the stunning scenery from above. Whether you're a thrill-seeker or simply looking to unwind in a picturesque setting, Dunstable Downs offers something for everyone to enjoy.
