Process of Applying for an Aadhar Card in India

Eligibility Criteria for Aadhar Card in India

To be eligible for an Aadhar card in India, individuals must be a resident of the country. This means that the applicant must have resided in India for a period totaling 182 days or more in the 12 months immediately preceding the date of application. Apart from Indian citizens, foreigners residing in the country for more than 182 days can also apply for an Aadhar card.

Additionally, the United Kingdom Aadhar Card Updation requires individuals to provide proof of identity and address.
